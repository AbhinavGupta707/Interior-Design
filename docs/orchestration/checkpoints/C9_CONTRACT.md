## Primary-source and first-principles decisions

- Multiway registration is a graph problem, not a pairwise averaging shortcut. Nodes represent source poses; reliable odometry/control-point edges and uncertain overlap/loop edges remain distinguishable; optimisation records information/residuals and may prune unreliable uncertain edges. [Open3D multiway registration](https://www.open3d.org/docs/release/tutorial/pipelines/multiway_registration.html)
- A free 3D similarity transform needs at least three independent non-collinear correspondences. Degenerate/rank-deficient anchors, reflection, non-finite input, overflow, invalid scale and disconnected components produce findings, partial registration or abstention.
- Robust estimation is deterministic for one frozen manifest: source order is canonicalised; seeds/config/tolerances and fixed-point output are versioned; residuals remain attached to each source. Consensus may identify inliers, but it may not average conflicting semantic claims into false agreement.
- Registration and semantic fitting are separate stages. Geometry kernels align bounded observations; the inference worker proposes parametric planes, walls, openings, rooms, levels, stairs and objects. Topology and C4 validation remain authoritative; invalid output is rejected or labelled unknown.
- Every result is compared with the exact committed existing-condition base snapshot. Source/model/tool/config hashes, coordinate frames, units, scale status, coverage, connected components, residuals, discrepancies and inference labels are durable.
- Fusion is acceptable only when, on deterministic synthetic truth with known limitations, it materially improves the declared primary geometric metrics over the best eligible single source without increasing severe errors. Otherwise the result is partial or abstained. Failures stay in the denominator.

## Frozen product, safety and mutation decisions

1. A request contains 2–32 immutable sources spanning at least two distinct source kinds. Every source has an exact reference/schema/hash, coordinate/scale state, provenance state, processing consent and training use fixed to denied. User assertions alone cannot establish a proposal.
2. The base is an exact existing-profile C4 snapshot ID/hash. Proposed/as-built profiles, stale hashes and cross-project/model sources fail closed.
3. Explicit anchors are source-scoped and unique. Project-local identity, validated anchors or robust constraint-graph registration are recorded per source. Disconnected sources never silently move into the largest component.
4. Fixed-point integer millimetres, parts-per-million scale and unit quaternion E9 are the durable transform boundary. Floating-point implementation details never leak into persisted equality or hashes.
5. Conflicts are first-class discrepancies with exact source claims, severity, location/magnitude when known, affected element IDs and possible typed C5 operations. Dimensional/topological/classification conflicts require at least two visible claims.
6. Occluded or unsupported geometry remains unknown or explicitly inferred with bounded confidence/evidence. Address/context data, appearance renders and neural/splat output cannot establish exact interior dimensions.
7. Jobs are tenant/project scoped, idempotent and versioned. Lease expiry/reclaim, cancellation at every stage, retry fencing, rights withdrawal and immutable atomic publication are required; stale workers cannot publish.
8. `fusion:proposal:review` stores optimistic, attributable human decisions. `fusion:proposal:draft` emits a branch/revision/head-hash-pinned C5 operation draft. It cannot call preview/commit, advance a branch or write canonical snapshots.
9. Owner/editor may create/read/cancel/retry/review/draft; viewer is read-only. Every foreign-tenant route denies before existence disclosure. Logs/traces carry safe codes, counts, hashes and stable IDs only.
10. Deterministic proposal fixtures keep C9 independently testable when physical RoomPlan, COLMAP/Open3D or CUDA are unavailable. Fixtures prove C9 logic only and remain visibly synthetic.

## Required checkpoint gate

1. `pnpm verify`, focused C9 contracts/authz/identity suites and all pre-existing suites pass. Strict schemas reject unknown fields, duplicate/stale/cross-model sources, insufficient kinds/anchors, consent/training violations, over-budget counts, inconsistent terminal references, invalid transforms/residuals and direct/empty mutation attempts.
2. Geometry property/severe-error suites cover exact identity/translation/rotation/scale, noisy/outlier anchors, canonical ordering, reflection, collinearity/rank deficiency, disconnected graphs, level mismatch, unit/scale conflict, overflow/non-finite input, deterministic hashes and abstention.
3. Semantic fitting tests cover bounded payloads, plane/wall/opening/room/level/stair/object relationships, invalid topology, overlapping/degenerate geometry, unsupported/occluded regions, confidence/provenance, deterministic output, cancellation/resource limits and zero canonical mutation.
4. A clean disposable PostGIS database applies C1-C9. Live API/worker tests prove tenant/project/model/source scope, idempotency, optimistic decisions, lease expiry/reclaim, cancellation at every stage, retry fencing, rights withdrawal, immutable publication, viewer read-only behavior, exact C5 draft pinning and no branch/snapshot mutation.
5. Independent evaluation declares synthetic dataset/split/metrics/tolerances and compares fusion with every eligible single-source baseline. Fusion must improve declared primary metrics or abstain; severe errors remain zero, failures remain in the denominator, confidence calibration is reported and correction-time is instrumented without fabricating human timing.
6. The integrated web journey runs through the real BFF/API/worker at desktop/mobile widths. Browser automation covers create/progress/cancel/retry, full/partial/disconnected/abstained proposals, source claims/residuals, accept/keep/correct/unknown/defer, stale conflict, exact operation draft, viewer denial, keyboard/accessibility, offline/error recovery, no overflow and zero unexpected console/network failures.
7. Computer Use or the in-app Browser is attempted for visible user evidence where available; deterministic Playwright is the documented fallback if the controller is unavailable. Synthetic/live-local status is labelled exactly.
8. Physical iPhone/RoomPlan accuracy and unavailable C8 COLMAP/Open3D/neural/CUDA runtime evidence remain `NOT RUN`. C9 fixtures and registration tests cannot promote those gates or claim representative home accuracy.
9. The ledger records task IDs, exact model/reasoning, worker/merge/product SHAs, commands/counts, live database/API/worker/browser evidence, integration repairs, residual limitations and final checkpoint state before C10 opens.
