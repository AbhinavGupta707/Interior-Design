# C8 Contract — Guided photo/video/RGB-D reconstruction

## Authority, outcome and scope

- Checkpoint: C8
- Immutable predecessor: C7 ledger-close commit `20f6c5f`
- Outcome: a user can capture or select rights-cleared photo, video or RGB-D evidence, follow explicit coverage guidance, start a durable reconstruction job, and receive privacy-reviewed frames, calibrated cameras, sparse/dense proposal geometry, diagnostics and an optional non-dimensional appearance layer.
- Contract versions: `c8-reconstruction-job-v1`, `c8-media-preparation-v1`, `c8-geometry-result-v1`, `c8-appearance-result-v1`, `c8-reconstruction-result-v1`
- Scope boundary: C8 reconstructs each media job independently. It does not silently merge disconnected models, resolve disagreements with plans or RoomPlan (C9), mutate canonical state, compile the deterministic browser scene (C10), infer hidden construction, or establish survey/professional truth.

The authoritative product and safety rules remain `AGENTS.md`, the accepted C4-C7 contracts and ADRs, and the active blue-sky execution plan. C8 narrows implementation without relaxing provenance, rights, tenant, typed-mutation, geometry or professional-status invariants. C7's unresolved physical RoomPlan release blocker remains open and cannot be converted into C8 evidence.

## Primary-source decisions

- COLMAP supports CPU feature extraction/matching when CUDA is disabled, while dense reconstruction requires CUDA in the documented pipeline. CPU operation may be materially slower and more memory intensive. The Mac lane therefore provides a real bounded sparse/adapter path and never reports CUDA/dense evidence unless an eligible NVIDIA run actually occurred. [COLMAP installation](https://colmap.github.io/install.html), [COLMAP FAQ](https://colmap.github.io/faq.html)
- COLMAP sparse outputs distinguish unordered/non-contiguous identifiers from contiguous indices; adapters may not index arrays by camera, image or point IDs. Disconnected submodels remain explicit. `model_merger` is allowed only when models share registered images, followed by global bundle adjustment. [COLMAP output format](https://colmap.github.io/format.html), [COLMAP CLI](https://colmap.github.io/cli.html), [COLMAP model alignment and merge](https://colmap.github.io/faq.html)
- A free 3D similarity alignment requires at least three independent correspondences and records its robust-estimation threshold and residuals. Fewer anchors leave scale/registration unknown; they never justify metric truth.
- Open3D's RGB-D pipeline registers/refines fragments and integrates known, validated intrinsics/poses into a TSDF from which point clouds or meshes can be extracted. A TSDF result records voxel/truncation/depth settings and cannot be created from invented poses. [Open3D reconstruction system](https://www.open3d.org/docs/release/tutorial/reconstruction_system/index.html), [Open3D TSDF integration](https://www.open3d.org/docs/release/tutorial/t_reconstruction_system/integration.html)
- Nerfstudio documents Linux as the recommended environment and GPU-enabled Docker/Windows workflows around NVIDIA CUDA. `ns-process-data` and `ns-export` can prepare data and export point/mesh artifacts, but C8 treats neural output as appearance and evaluation evidence only. [Nerfstudio installation](https://docs.nerf.studio/quickstart/installation.html), [custom data](https://docs.nerf.studio/quickstart/custom_dataset.html), [CLI](https://docs.nerf.studio/reference/cli/index.html), [geometry export](https://docs.nerf.studio/quickstart/export_geometry.html)
- gsplat is CUDA accelerated and its published pre-built environments target pinned Python/PyTorch/CUDA combinations. Its splats never become dimensional authority. [gsplat repository and installation](https://github.com/nerfstudio-project/gsplat)
- FFmpeg frame selection and filters are invoked through fixed argument builders with bounded inputs, timeout/output limits and no shell. Exact executable versions, filter configuration and source hashes enter every preparation manifest. [FFmpeg filter documentation](https://www.ffmpeg.org/ffmpeg-filters.html)

## Frozen product, privacy and geometry decisions

1. Native iOS guided capture is part of C8, not deferred. It supports photo/video on eligible devices and optional RGB-D capture only when the exact device/runtime capability is present. Permission denial, interruption, thermal/resource pressure, insufficient coverage and unavailable depth have accessible recovery routes. Simulator fixtures are visibly synthetic and cannot prove camera, depth, tracking, thermal or background behavior.
2. Existing C2 immutable assets remain the source boundary. Native capture uploads through the existing rights-first, checksum-bound evidence mechanism; C8 public jobs refer only to immutable asset IDs, source hashes, sizes and detected media types. They never expose object keys, local paths, source payloads, signed URLs or credentials.
3. Service-processing consent is required and training use is fixed to `denied`. Rights withdrawal and cancellation are rechecked before lease and publication. Raw customer media, sanitized frames, model weights and runtime caches never enter Git, telemetry, fixtures or crash reports.
4. Media preparation is deterministic for one pinned manifest: validate decoded type/duration/dimensions, sample bounded frames, normalize orientation/color, strip metadata, compute blur/exposure/overlap diagnostics and apply a pluggable face/text/privacy redaction review hook. A `review-required` or rejected privacy result cannot enter reconstruction as accepted.
5. No worker accepts arbitrary flags, executable paths, shell strings, URLs or object keys from a request. Subprocesses run from allowlisted adapters in bounded temporary workspaces with fixed environment, wall-clock/CPU/memory/output limits, cancellation and cleanup. Logs contain stable IDs, stages, safe codes and hashes only.
6. Geometry and appearance are separate manifests. Geometry artifacts are `proposal-only`; Nerfstudio/gsplat viewer, radiance and splat artifacts are `non-dimensional`. Appearance cannot provide metric scale, overwrite cameras/geometry, enter C4 as truth or hide geometric abstention.
7. Geometry scale is exactly `metric-validated`, `metric-estimated` or `unknown`. Unknown scale stays in arbitrary units. Validated alignment requires at least three independent correspondences plus recorded residuals; sensor-estimated metric scale remains labelled estimated until external validation.
8. Sparse/dense components, registered/input frame counts, cameras, coordinate handedness, scale, tool/config/source hashes and diagnostics remain explicit. Partial registration, disconnected components, poor calibration, excessive residuals or invalid poses produce visible findings or abstention rather than a silently cropped success.
9. RGB SfM, RGB-D TSDF and hybrid modes are explicit. Open3D TSDF needs validated intrinsics/poses. COLMAP IDs remain map keys, never array positions. Coordinate conversion and geometry parsing reject non-finite values, unbounded counts, malformed binaries/text and path traversal.
10. A reconstruction result is immutable and idempotent for the exact request/source/tool/config hashes. Retry creates a fenced attempt; late/stale workers cannot publish. C8 does not call C5 preview/commit. C9 alone may register C8 proposals with other evidence and present discrepancies for confirmation.

## Bounded contract and route inventory

The shared schemas in `packages/contracts/src/c8.ts` freeze:

- 512 source assets, 100 GiB aggregate input, 20 GiB per source, 10,000 prepared frames, 50 megapixels per frame, 64 result artifacts and three attempts;
- exact RGB/RGB-D/hybrid and disabled/optional-appearance modes;
- immutable source IDs, sizes, detected types and SHA-256 hashes with explicit rights;
- zero registration anchors or at least three independent correspondence anchors, never one or two;
- exact job/terminal-state/result-reference invariants and safe failure codes;
- stripped-frame privacy/quality manifests, source/tool/config hashes and bounded integer metrics;
- explicit component/frame/scale/unit/alignment diagnostics; and
- proposal-only geometry versus non-dimensional appearance authority.

Frozen public routes:

- `GET|POST /v1/projects/:projectId/reconstruction-jobs`
- `GET /v1/projects/:projectId/reconstruction-jobs/:reconstructionJobId`
- `POST /v1/projects/:projectId/reconstruction-jobs/:reconstructionJobId/cancel`
- `POST /v1/projects/:projectId/reconstruction-jobs/:reconstructionJobId/retry`
- `GET /v1/projects/:projectId/reconstruction-jobs/:reconstructionJobId/result`

Owner/editor may create, read, cancel and retry jobs and read results. Viewer may read jobs/results only. Every foreign-tenant decision denies before resource disclosure; all mutations require exact idempotency and optimistic/fenced state. No role can use C8 to confirm or mutate canonical geometry.
